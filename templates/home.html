Who are we?

IEEE at the University of Maryland (IEEE@UMD) serves to cater the needs of both undergraduate and graduate Electrical and Computer engineers within the Clark School of Engineering. This includes providing professional, social, and academic resources to our members, as well as a connection to the international IEEE community.
