About Us

Introduction

Welcome!

This website is dedicated to the Institute of Electrical and Electronics (IEEE) chapter at the University of Maryland, College Park. A student-led organization, IEEE caters to th needs of both undergraduate and graduate Electrical and Computer Engineering students within the Clark School of Engineering. This includes providing professional, social, and academic resources to our members, as well as a connection to the international IEEE community.

If you have any questions, information to share, or suggestions, please do not hesitate to contact us at:

ieee.umd@gmail.com

Academics

One of the most-valued academic resources that IEEE@UMD has to offer is our testbank. Our testbank offers a large number of old exams for every ENEE class and professor that has taught at UMD. Aside from this, we also have student lounge located in Glen L. Martin, Room 0104. The lounge provides students with a relaxing and academically-thriving student environment to either take a break and or study during any time of the day. If you wish to have access to our lounge, please send us an email.

Social and Professional Opportunities

Some examples of events that we hold throughout the school semester include:

  • Engineering company information sessions
  • S’mores Night
  • Tutoring Sessions
  • Professional Happy Hour
  • Field Day
  • Fundraising Nights
  • IEEE Region 2 Student Activities Conference
  • Colors of ECE
  • Maryland Day

Check out the rest of our website for other resources, such our events calendar, sponsorship package, and contact information. Don’t forget to subscribe to our listserv for our e-mails!

--Kelly Fernandez, IEEE@UMD President 2015-2016
